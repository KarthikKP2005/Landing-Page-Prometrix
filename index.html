ABOUT US

Central Manufacturing Technology Institute

CMTI is a premier R&D organization focusing on providing cutting-edge technology solutions to the manufacturing sector. Our state-of-the-art facilities and expert team drive innovation in Industry 4.0 implementation.

At Prometrix, we operate at the intersection of mathematical theory and robust industrial execution. Founded by a dedicated cohort of computational physicists and automation engineers, we specialize in translating complex parametric specifications into direct, hardware-native orchestration.

Our philosophy rejects superficial technological overhead. Instead, we deliver light, highly performant, G-Code generated pipelines and custom telemetry solutions built on low-latency mathematical architecture, ensuring high precision for global defense, aerospace, and advanced manufacturing sectors.
